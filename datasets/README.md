Dataset Sources
===============

1. The csv2hpp utility is available at: https://github.com/fleschutz/csv2hpp
2. chemical_elements.h has been converted from chemical_elements.csv by csv2hpp (downloaded from https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/berkayalan/chemical-periodic-table-elements under Other license).
3. exoplanets.h has been converted from exoplanets.csv by csv2hpp (produced by the NASA Exoplanet Archive: http://exoplanetarchive.ipac.caltech.edu on Mon Jan 12 06:51:06 2026).
4. planets.h has been converted from planets.csv by csv2hpp.
5. natural_satellites.h has been converted from natural_satellites.csv by csv2hpp (downloaded from https://github.com/devstronomy/nasa-data-scraper under MIT license). See also https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_natural_satellites.
6. sunspots.h has been converted from sunspots.csv by csv2hpp (downloaded from https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/robervalt/sunspots under CC0 Public Domain license).
